# Benefique Paid Ads — Creative Bank v1

**Audience:** SE FL SMB owners, $1–10M revenue, owner-operated (impact-glass, marine, MSP, med/dental/vet practices, import-export, 2nd-gen manufacturing).
**Positioning:** Book-replacement — flat-fee CFO + tax + bookkeeping that replaces a ~$236K/yr in-house finance stack (6 skill-sets no one person holds) at ~38% of the cost.
**Offer (the lead magnet = the "appointment"):** Free Benefique Diagnostic — we read your books and hand you a one-page Matrix + Three Views + cash-flow waterfall. Same methodology delivered to Paletz, Zyis, Titan.

## Brand & compliance rails (do not violate)
- **Lead with the flat monthly fee.** % of revenue is reference-only — NEVER imply we bill on revenue.
- **Don't overclaim industry experience.** Cite real comps from the book (e.g., Titan for job-cost). Frame niche knowledge as "calibrated during onboarding," not "we're experts in your industry."
- **Don't link `cfo.benefique.com` publicly.** Send paid traffic to the landing page / booking funnel only.
- **Signature on any owner-facing asset:** Gerrit Disbergen, EA (no middle initial, no other creds).
- **Persuade with the in-house contrast**, not fear. The number does the work.
- Every ad ends in the SAME action: tap → landing page → form → <2-min text-back → booked diagnostic call.
