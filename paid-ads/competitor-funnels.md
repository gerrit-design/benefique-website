## 1. PHG Advisory — the gold-standard funnel (running since Oct 2023)
**Ad → LP:** `https://phgadvisory.com/cfo-bookkeeping-meta/` (a *dedicated* Meta landing page, not their homepage)

**Structure to steal, top to bottom:**
1. **Qualifier eyebrow:** *"Exclusively for Family- and Founder-Owned Businesses Doing $1M+ in Annual Revenue"* — filters before they read. (We'd say SE FL owner-operated, $1–10M.)
2. **Value headline:** *"Get a Fractional CFO + Bookkeeping Team… Without the Cost of Hiring In House."* — **literally our in-house contrast.**
3. **One-line offer terms:** *"one flat monthly fee, no long-term contracts, guaranteed 24-hour response."* — flat fee + reassurance in a single line.
4. **Primary CTA (repeated 4×):** *"Schedule Your Complimentary Assessment"* — the free-diagnostic mechanic, named.
5. **Testimonials** from trade/service SMBs (roofing ×2, garage, security) — note: **roofers again** (full circle to the original playbook).
6. **Scope-of-work table** (18 checkmarks: cleanup → accrual → close → KPIs → forecast → sale prep) — exactly your "$0 included stack."
7. **Case studies** in a fixed template — *"How We Helped a $XM Business [outcome]"* → Challenge / What We Did / Outcome / Key Takeaway. Several mirror YOUR stories almost verbatim:
   - *"Correct Financials That Showed a False Loss"* (−$1M EBITDA → actually +$2.1M; prepayment/revenue-recognition error) = your **"December financials are lying" / "profitable but broke."**
   - *"Rebuild Clarity After a Failed Acquisition"* ($11M vs real $6M EBITDA killed an $80M deal) = your **buyer/diligence lens.**
   - *"$35M Business Reduce Payment Processing Costs ($100K saved)"* = your **trapped-cash / found-money** angle.
8. **FAQ** (8 Q's incl. *"How do fees work?"* → "fixed monthly fee, not tied to hours, month-to-month") — feeds their GEO/schema, same play as your `q`/`a` FAQ strategy.
9. **Form:** Typeform qualifier — Q1 *"Are you a Business Owner?"* with the disqualifier option literally written *"No (We only service Business Owners)"*, Q2 industry.

**Verdict:** This is the template. Benefique already has every ingredient (flat fee, included stack, the exact case studies, FAQ/schema discipline). The build is assembling them into one dedicated `/cfo-diagnostic` page.

## 2. AURA Accounting — the niche-domination funnel
**Ad → LP:** `https://agency.auracfo.com/fb` (dedicated, niched to **marketing agencies**)

**Structure to steal:**
1. **Headline names the niche + the core tension:** *"Marketing Agencies: it's time to grow profit, not just revenue."*
2. **Proof stat bar** (instant credibility): *76+ active clients · $176M revenues under management · 35% average client savings.* — Benefique can run an anonymized version (clients served, $ under management, avg trapped-cash found).
3. **Problem-agitate, 4 cards:** Cash Flow Chaos · The Profitability Mystery · The Hiring Gamble · Client Concentration Risk. → maps to your **SPOF/concentration**, **per-client profitability**, **cash-vs-profit** content.
4. **"Profitable on paper, broke in reality"** = your S2 angle, almost word-for-word.
5. **3-step method:** Clean Up → Build Command Center → Guide Growth.
6. **Offer name:** *"No BS Profit Audit"* — sharper than "free diagnostic." Steal the *spirit*.
7. **5-step "Getting Started"** with explicit timelines: Intro Call (30 min) → Profit Audit (48–72 hrs) → Tailored Proposal (48 hrs, flat monthly fee) → Onboarding (1–3 wks) → Monthly Cadence. — sets expectations, reduces friction.
8. **Pricing block:** *"Flat monthly fee. 30-day out clause. No surprises. We earn your business every month."*
9. **FAQ** opens with *"How is AURA different from my current bookkeeper?"* = your **"'I just need a bookkeeper' trap"** (V5).
10. **Form:** Name / Agency / Phone / Email + reCAPTCHA. (Phone field = their speed-to-lead hook.)

**Verdict:** The blueprint for your healthcare/imaging vertical page. Swap "agency" → "imaging center / concierge practice," swap the 4 pains → DSO/payor-mix/PET-toxic-scan/modality-margin.

## 3. Tembo CPAs — the teaching counter-example (also Oct 2023)
**Ad → LP:** `https://tembocpas.com/` — **their generic homepage, NOT a dedicated landing page.**

Despite 20-month ad longevity, Tembo dumps paid clicks onto a homepage ("Built by Entrepreneurs for Entrepreneurs," services grid, "Why Tembo" name story). No qualifier, no single CTA, no case studies above the fold, no lead-capture form on landing.

**Lesson:** Longevity ≠ optimized funnel. Tembo wins on *ad volume + 20-month persistence*, and is **leaving money on the table at the landing step** — which is exactly the gap you beat them on with a PHG/AURA-style dedicated page + speed-to-lead bot.

## 4. STAXX (`staxx.cfo` / startstaxx.pro) — the brand-new, well-crafted clone of OUR model
**Page:** facebook.com/STAXXCFO/ · **LP:** `https://www.startstaxx.pro/` (ClickFunnels + Wistia VSL) · **~21 ad variants, ALL started May 25–27, 2026 (≈1 week old).**

**Read:** Not a longevity winner yet (don't over-weight him as "validated" — he has ~1 week and "low impression" tags on several ads). But the funnel *craft* is excellent and his positioning is **almost identical to Benefique's**, so he's the single most relevant competitor to study.

### His ad (one long-form creative, ~21 near-identical variants)
- **Hook:** *"Tired of working with an accounting firm that never gets back to you?"*
- **Reframe:** *"The best way… is NOT an end-of-year tax accountant, an overseas bookkeeper, or an in-house accountant. It's plugging in a fractional accounting and finance team."*
- **Bundle:** *"a dedicated fractional CFO, Tax CPA, AND bookkeepers for just ONE monthly price."*
- **Differentiator:** *"We use Slack, and guarantee 24 hrs or less response times."*
- **4× "No more…"** pain stack (unresponsive accountants / doing the work yourself / flying blind / overpaying for in-house — *the in-house contrast*).
- **CTA mechanic:** *"click Learn More, put your information in, then watch the video on the next page"* → opt-in → VSL.

### His landing page / VSL (5:55) — the full playbook
1. **Two-step qualifier FIRST** (same as PHG): *"Are you a business owner? Yes / No (We ONLY service business owners)"* + industry. Disqualify before the pitch.
2. **Headline:** *"Get A Fractional CFO & Accounting Team For One Flat Monthly Price… Make Smarter Decisions, Scale Faster, & Save Wasted Revenue."*
3. **VSL beats (all transferable):**
   - **Risk reversal:** *"complete clarity… or we won't keep working with you."*
   - **Take-away / premium exclusion:** *"If your business is constantly tight on cash… this is NOT the right fit. We're not here to save struggling businesses — we optimize businesses already generating real revenue."*
   - **The "patchwork not a system" gap:** *"a bookkeeper logging transactions, a CPA handling taxes… but none of it answers 'what should I do next?'"* → our exact "books that are done vs. useful" angle.
   - **Profitable-but-unclear:** *"Revenue's coming in, looks profitable, but cash isn't predictable."* = our S2.
   - **No-niche-overclaim (identical to our rail!):** *"We're not trying to be experts in your niche — you already are. We specialize in financial clarity, cash management, decision systems."*
   - **Signature deliverable:** a **rolling 13-week cash-flow forecast** + private Slack + 24-hr response.
   - **Retention proof:** *"~90 active clients, most staying well over a year."*
   - **PRICE STATED IN THE VSL:** *"engagements typically start around $4,000/month and scale with complexity"* — then qualifies on affordability.
   - **Close:** short application → book a call.
4. **"Here's What You Get" grid:** know your numbers · dedicated CFO+team one flat rate · we help you decide · plain-English CFO reporting · <24-hr Slack · access to vetted tax & lending partners.

### How Benefique BEATS STAXX (he just handed us the gaps)
- **Speed:** his funnel is opt-in → 5:55 VSL → application → *book a call* (slow). Our **<2-min SMS text-back** contacts and books while he's still asking them to watch a video.
- **Local + vertical depth:** he's explicitly generic ("not experts in your niche"). We are SE FL + real radiology/imaging/healthcare depth (DSO, PET, modality margin) → a sharper, higher-trust niche page (the AURA move he didn't make).
- **Proof:** he claims ~90 clients generically; we can show *anonymized real numbers* ($353K trapped cash, the Matrix, dashboards the client can verify).
- **Price transparency as a weapon:** he buries "$4k/mo" 5 min into a VSL. We can state the flat-fee + in-house contrast up front (more GEO-friendly, less friction).
- **What to STEAL from him:** the two-step disqualifier gate, the 13-week cash-flow forecast as a named signature deliverable, the take-away/premium-exclusion framing, and the "patchwork not a system" line.
