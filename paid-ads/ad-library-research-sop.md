# SOP — Facebook Ad Library Research (find proven winners to model)

**Goal:** Find ads that have been running 6+ months (= profitable, or they'd have been killed) in adjacent categories, then model their *structure* — hook, pacing, offer mechanics, CTA — onto Benefique's offer. We model the skeleton, never the claims.

**Why this works:** A B2B-services ad nobody can copy your numbers from, but everybody can copy the *winning shape*. Longevity is the only public proxy for "this ad makes money."

## Where
Facebook Ad Library: https://www.facebook.com/ads/library/ (set Country = United States, Ad category = "All ads").

## Who to study (adjacent, not identical)
Benefique has no impulse-roofing analog, so model **high-consideration local B2B services** that have cracked Meta:
1. **Fractional CFO / outsourced accounting firms** (search: "fractional CFO", "bookkeeping for", "outsourced accounting", "CFO services")
2. **Tax resolution / tax planning firms** (search: "tax planning", "save on taxes business owner", "S-corp")
3. **Local B2B lead-gen agencies & SaaS for SMB owners** (search: "for small business owners", "QuickBooks", "cash flow")
4. **Adjacent pro services that advertise heavily** — law firms, insurance, wealth/RIA (their trust-building structure transfers well to accounting)

## The filter (this is the whole game)
For each candidate ad, record only if **ALL** are true:
- [ ] Running **180+ days** ("Started running on" date is shown on each ad — count back from today)
- [ ] **Multiple active variations** of the same concept (signals real spend + iteration)
- [ ] Targets **business owners / decision-makers**, not consumers
- [ ] Has a **clear single CTA** (lead form, "book a call", "free X")

## What to capture per winner (paste into the log table below)
1. **Hook** — the first line / first 1.5s. Verbatim.
2. **Offer mechanic** — free audit? calculator? guide? call? (what they trade for the contact)
3. **Format** — static / carousel / video / lead-form-native
4. **Proof device** — number, screenshot, testimonial, guarantee
5. **CTA** — exact button + destination type (on-platform lead form vs. landing page)
6. **Days running** + **# variations**
7. **The Benefique remap** — one line: how this shape maps to our offer (which `ad-copy-variants.md` concept it reinforces or inspires)

## The remap rule (compliance)
- Model **structure**, not substance. Copy the *cadence* of "Number → Pain → Mechanism → Free offer," never their figures, client names, or guarantees.
- Every remapped claim must be true for Benefique and pass the brand rails in `ad-copy-variants.md` (lead with flat fee, no industry overclaim, no `cfo.benefique.com`).
- If a winner's offer is "free audit," ours is the **free Benefique Diagnostic** — same mechanic, our methodology.

## Cadence
- **Round 1 (now):** 10 winners → fills/validates the v1 creative bank.
- **Monthly:** 5 fresh winners. Refilling the bank is how you beat creative fatigue (the #1 reason Meta CPL climbs).
