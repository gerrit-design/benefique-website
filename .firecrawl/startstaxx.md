# Frequently Asked Questions

How Is This Different From My Current CPA or Bookkeeper?

Your bookkeeper's job is to get data into QuickBooks. Your CPA's job is to file taxes and keep you compliant. Neither one is responsible for helping you run the business going forward. That's why even when everything looks fine on paper, it still feels unclear.

Our job is to run the financial side of your business so it makes more money and never runs out of cash. We're CFO-first. We rebuild your books around how you actually operate, install a 13-week cash flow forecast so you have a clear view of your cash position, and give you CFO reports and advisory on pricing, hiring, debt, and profit.

Bookkeeper plus CPA equals compliance. STAXX is the financial command center that drives profit and cash.

What Does This Monthly Fee Include?

The ongoing retainer covers your installed fractional CFO and accounting team, all CFO services, ongoing accounting, the 13-week cash flow forecast, our exclusive tax directory, and a guaranteed 24-hour-or-less response time in your private Slack channel.

It also includes the one-time setup and cleanup at no separate fee. That covers onboarding, books cleanup, our 76-step roadmap for bulletproof financials, weekly calls with your accounting cleanup specialist, and your initial CFO sprint (four weekly calls with your fractional CFO). In the past we've charged $7,500 to $15,000 for this piece. Today we bundle it into the retainer as part of the relationship.

Do I Need To Fire My Existing Accountant or Bookkeeper?

Not necessarily. It depends on what you have and the size of your company.

If your bookkeeper is a rock star, we can review their work and layer our fractional CFO services on top. If they're not cutting it, our team can take over the accounting.

For larger companies with an internal bookkeeper, we often recommend shifting that role to focus on AR, AP, collections, and payroll, while we take over transaction categorization, reconciliations, and month-end close and review.

On the tax side, we typically coordinate with your existing CPA, or plug you into one of our vetted tax partners if you're looking for a change.

Either way, you get one financial head making sure everyone is rowing in the same direction.

What If I'm Planning To Sell My Business In The Next Few Years?

We have specific expertise in preparing businesses for exit. We'll help ensure your books are pristine, your financial history is well-documented, and there won't be any red flags during due diligence that could reduce your valuation or kill the deal.

Is There A Long-Term Contract?

We start with a 90-day agreement that converts to month-to-month after that, with a simple 30-day notice to cancel. That gives us enough runway to do real work and produce real results, without ever locking you into a long-term contract if we're not delivering value. That's our version of a guarantee: deliver value every month, or you're free to leave.
