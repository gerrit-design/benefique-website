## Who we are

## Not your average CPA Firm

We help you define, and achieve, your success

Tembo was built by entrepreneurs, for entrepreneurs.

Yes, we are a CPA firm; but we’re not just your average firm. What can you expect from us?

- Proactive tax planning
- Handling tax compliance matters
- Accounting & bookkeeping
- Helping you understand your financial reporting systems
- Budgeting, financial modeling, and financial analysis
- Helping you understand valuation, cash flow, and capital structures.
- And, we pride ourselves on actually creating a relationship with you.

But what makes Tembo different is the entrepreneurial experience we bring to the table. We know what it’s like to walk in your shoes. We’re serious about clients that are serious about their business.
