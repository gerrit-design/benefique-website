## Why Most Agencies Are Profitable On Paper, But Broke In Reality

### Cash Flow Chaos

You're billing progressively across projects that span weeks or months, creating unpredictable cash flow gaps. You never know if you can safely invest in that new hire or marketing campaign.

### The Profitability Mystery

Which clients are making you money? Which projects are bleeding you dry? Cash-based accounting can't tell you, so you're flying blind on the decisions that matter most.

### The Hiring Gamble

Should you hire full-time, part-time, or freelance? Without real-time labor cost tracking against project revenue, every hiring decision feels like a coin flip.

### Client Concentration Risk

You're too dependent on 2 to 3 major clients. If one leaves, your revenue crumbles, but you don't have the financial visibility to diversify safely.

## What Other Agency Owners Wanted to Know

How is AURA different from my current bookkeeper?

Bookkeepers record transactions. We provide controller-level accounting with strategic CFO guidance. We track project profitability, forecast cash flow, and help you make growth decisions, not just balance the books.

I'm only doing $2M in revenue. Am I too small?

Not at all. Our sweet spot is $1M to $5M agencies that are growing fast and need financial clarity to scale safely. If you're feeling cash flow pressure or don't know your true profitability, we can help.

What if I'm already working with a bookkeeper?

Perfect. We can collaborate with your existing bookkeeper (if they're good) or take over entirely (if they're not). Either way, we'll upgrade your financial infrastructure to the controller and CFO level.

How involved do I need to be?

As much or as little as you want. Most clients spend 30 to 60 minutes per month in a strategy call and respond to occasional questions via email or text. We handle the heavy lifting.

Can I cancel if it's not working?

Yes. We have a 30-day out clause because we earn your trust every single month. No long-term contracts, no hard feelings if we're not the right fit.

How do you handle different billing models?

This is our specialty. We structure your accounting to match your business model, whether you bill hourly, project-based, retainer, or performance-based (or a mix). You'll see profitability by client and billing type.
